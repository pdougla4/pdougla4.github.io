Senior Design — Red River UHS Operating Envelope analysis complete

Built a temperature-aware Safe Operating Envelope (SOE) for underground H₂ storage in the Red River A–C intervals (Williston Basin): allowable (q, ΔT) combinations satisfying three guardbands — AIG (Anhydrite Integrity Guard: caprock thermo-mechanical integrity, thermal effects embedded), PGN (capillary seal stability and fingering resistance), and RMG (Reservoir Mechanical Guard: near-wellbore thermal embrittlement). A No-False-Green policy enforces P90 conservative boundaries; regions near thresholds derate to amber/red automatically. A secondary sub-project evaluates the same formation as a CO₂-EOR target — quantifying incremental recovery and sequestration potential using CMG/GEM compositional simulation.

Show technical details
  • Kernel: Closed-form Warren–Root dual-porosity + heat (Python) — Stehfest inversion for pressure, erfc analytical solution for caprock thermal attenuation, periodic steady-state for cycle thermal memory. No numerical PDE solvers.
  • Guardbands: AIG (Mohr-Coulomb / Hoek-Brown caprock screen, FoS = 1.75, H₂ chemical embrittlement per Aluah et al. 2025); PGN (Brooks-Corey drainage, J-scaled Pc, M / Ca / G stability regimes); RMG (reservoir carbonate thermal embrittlement, FoS = 1.5).
  • UQ: LHS on broad priors → physical pre-filter → Morris screening → Sobol analysis on reduced parameter set at representative (q, ΔT) points. Key finding: caprock pore throat radius drives ~85% of total-order sensitivity — flagged as primary limitation pending direct measurement.
  • Lab inputs: Hassler brine permeability, NMR T₂, Amott wettability, sessile-drop contact angle, pendant-drop IFT. Red River core plugs and caprock plates from three wells.
  • CO₂-EOR: CMG/GEM sector model (Red River A); +15.8% incremental oil recovery, 13.7 BCF storage capacity, 104–109 M lb CO₂ sequestered over 30-year horizon.
  • Note: TH2M / MINC anchors were planned for stress-coupling validation but dropped due to time constraints; conservatism is maintained through explicit FoS margins and the P90 boundary policy.
