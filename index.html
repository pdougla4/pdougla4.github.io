Senior Design in progress

Non-isothermal TH₂M operating envelopes (rate × BHP) for cyclic H₂ in fractured Red River carbonates, with P10/P50/P90 bands and a thermal-stress margin. Lab core-plug tests (intact vs. kerfed) will estimate dual-porosity λ (shape factor) and ω (storage ratio); those parameters feed the model. Uncertainty handled with LHS/QMC and Sobol’ indices; outputs include chance-constrained operating points.

Show technical plan
  • Physics & outputs: Non-isothermal TH₂M; operating envelopes (rate × BHP) with P10/P50/P90 bands and a thermal-stress margin.
  • Lab → model: Core-plug testing (intact vs. kerfed) to infer dual-porosity λ and ω by facies, with uncertainty carried into the model.
  • Stack: OpenGeoSys (TH₂M) driven from Python; small CMG cross-check on matched scenarios.
  • UQ: LHS/QMC sampling; global sensitivity (Morris/Sobol’); chance-constrained picks (e.g., P(safe) ≥ 0.9) and a Value-of-Information ranking.
  • Reproducibility: run manifests (CSV) and exact CLI; headless runs; CSV post-processing.
